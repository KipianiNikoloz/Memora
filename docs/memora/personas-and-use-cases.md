# Personas and Use Cases

## Primary Persona: Reflective Achiever

The reflective achiever is a student or young professional who wants to track progress and quiet negative self-talk. They need quick capture, gentle encouragement, and a low-effort way to notice personal growth when time and emotional energy are limited.

## Secondary Persona: Founder Under Pressure

The founder under pressure is building through uncertainty and needs help remembering milestones, setbacks, and moments of resilience. They benefit from an archive that makes progress visible during stressful or inconsistent routines.

## Secondary Persona: People Manager

The people manager or HR stakeholder wants structured reflection to support growth conversations. This persona requires explicit privacy, trust, and consent boundaries. Organizational use must remain secondary until the individual-user experience is mature.

## MVP Use Cases

- Capture a meaningful life moment.
- Record emotion, tags, life phase, and reflection.
- Receive an AI-generated chapter title or reflective response.
- Browse memories by shelf, emotion, tag, or life phase.
- Revisit old entries to compare past and present perspective.
- View lightweight insights about emotional patterns and milestone highlights.

## Future Use Cases

- Founder milestone archive for resilience during startup uncertainty.
- Wellness or productivity integrations that prompt reflection without overwhelming the user.
- Organizational solutions for private employee growth reflection, only with strong privacy and consent controls.
