## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Experience audit
The system SHALL verify MVP screens against the documented Memora UX and design system.

#### Scenario: Experience passes
- **WHEN** the audit reviews public and app screens
- **THEN** the screens match the warm, structured, reflective Memora direction and remain usable on mobile and desktop

### Requirement: Safety and privacy audit
The system SHALL verify AI safety, privacy copy, and sensitive-data controls.

#### Scenario: Safety passes
- **WHEN** the audit tests AI and privacy flows
- **THEN** the app avoids clinical claims, keeps tone user-controlled, and accurately explains private data behavior

### Requirement: Readiness remediation
The system SHALL fix experience, safety, privacy, accessibility, or state gaps discovered during the audit.

#### Scenario: Readiness gap is found
- **WHEN** the audit finds a blocking MVP issue
- **THEN** the change remediates it before demo readiness work begins
