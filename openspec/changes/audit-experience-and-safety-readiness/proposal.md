## Why

After AI, insights, public UI, and privacy settings are in place, the MVP needs a dedicated pass for user experience quality, safety, privacy, accessibility, and visual fidelity.

## What Changes

- Audit product experience against the knowledge base.
- Fix design drift, safety issues, privacy gaps, responsive problems, and missing state handling.
- Do not add new product scope beyond readiness fixes.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `experience-safety-readiness-audit`: Audits and remediates UX, safety, privacy, accessibility, and design readiness gaps.

### Modified Capabilities

- None.

## Impact

- May update UI, copy, tests, AI safety handling, settings, or state components.
