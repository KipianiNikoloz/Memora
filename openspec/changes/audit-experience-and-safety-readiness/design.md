## Context

This audit follows the core product, AI, insights, public UI, and settings specs.

## Goals / Non-Goals

**Goals:**

- Verify design fidelity and responsive behavior.
- Verify AI safety and privacy boundaries.
- Verify empty/loading/error states across MVP screens.
- Fix readiness gaps before demo prep.

**Non-Goals:**

- No new major features.
- No full production compliance program.
- No organization use cases.

## Decisions

- Use `docs/memora/` as the checklist source.
- Treat safety/privacy copy accuracy as a blocking issue.
- Include accessibility basics in the audit.

## Risks / Trade-offs

- Visual polish can consume time -> Prioritize issues that affect comprehension, trust, or usability.
